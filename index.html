With DAW software, music can be made with clips from across the country and even across the world! Just have clips of the instrumentals, vocals, etc to combine them and start making the music. The first DAW came around the 70s and the 80s. You can make countless songs with DAW easily! Anyone I mean ANYONE is capable of making music from their bathrooms, parks and from anywhere really. It contains standard computer features like copy, paste, etc which is extremely convenient for music makers!

A USB microphone is a very portable device that helps music makers record high quality recordings connected to any device such as your laptop, computer, etc!

DAWs work together with audio interfaces. The works of DAWs, despite their abilities, depend so much on the audio interface hardware.
